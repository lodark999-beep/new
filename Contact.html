Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

The minimum order quantity varies by product. For most models, we accept orders as low as 2-19 units. Please contact us for specific MOQ requirements.

Do you provide custom branding options?

Yes, we offer OEM/ODM services with custom branding options. We can customize the software interface, hardware specifications, and packaging to meet your requirements.

What is your warranty policy?

We provide a 12-month warranty for all our products. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and includes technical support.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping time depends on your location and the shipping method chosen. Typically, it takes 15-30 days for sea freight and 5-10 days for air freight.
