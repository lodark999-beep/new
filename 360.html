5 Intelligent Assistive Modes

Reverse Mode

Activates automatically in reverse gear, displaying dynamic guidance lines with video storage capability.

  • Automatic activation when reversing
  • Dynamic guidance lines
  • Video recording and storage
  • Enhanced safety during parking

3D Surround Mode

Automatically activates 360° view upon vehicle start-up for complete surrounding awareness.

  • 360° panoramic view
  • Automatic activation at startup
  • Complete surrounding awareness
  • Eliminates blind spots

Pull Over Mode

Activates narrow space assist when parking, showing both vehicle sides simultaneously to prevent tire and body damage.

  • Narrow space assistance
  • Simultaneous side view
  • Prevents tire and body damage
  • Perfect for tight parking spots

Starting Mode

Forward view activates when front sensors detect obstacles, helping prevent forward collisions.

  • Forward obstacle detection
  • Automatic front view activation
  • Collision prevention
  • Enhanced forward safety

Turning Mode

Provides expanded view during turns with dynamic guide lines for optimal path correction.

  • Expanded turning view
  • Dynamic guide lines
  • Optimal path correction
  • Enhanced turning safety
