Common Issues & Solutions

  • DVR Plug Damage Issues

    We have identified multiple issues affecting units due to a damaged DVR plug.

    Solution:

    Inspect the DVR plug for any physical damage. If damaged, contact customer support for replacement instructions.

    Note: Continuing to use a damaged plug may cause further issues with your system.
  • WiFi Connected but No Internet

    MY WIFI is connected, 4G antenna and F port antennas are also plugged in but I have no internet.

    Solution:

    Make sure that the time and date are correct on the unit.

  • GPS Signal Problems

    My unit is installed and the GPS is not receiving signal.

    Solution:

    Check that the connection is not too tight! This is a common occurrence.

    Note: GPS performance can be affected by weather conditions and building density.
  • Vehicle Doors and Dynamic Reverse Lines Not Working

    My vehicle car doors and dynamic reverse lines are not working.

    Solution:

    If your original head unit did not show this, it generally will not carry over as this is a vehicle option.

  • Screen Cut Off by Frame

    I have a frame that installs on top of the screen, however some of the screen is now cut off.

    Solution:

    There are settings for this found here.

    My door graphics and A/C display used to work but now it is not showing.

Power & Connection Issues

  • Power Issues - Full Boot Instead of Quick Boot

    The unit does a full boot instead of quick boot every time the accessories is turned on.

    Solution:

    The Accessories G3 and Main battery power G2 are swapped around so that G2 is coming on with ignition and G3 is always on.

    G1= 0VDC NEGATIVE
    G2= 12VDC ACCESSORIES POWER (Swapped with G3)
    G3= 12VDC MAIN BATTERY (Swapped with G2)

    Correct Vehicle Voltage condition on these pins when ignition is ON should be as follows:

    G1= 0VDC NEGATIVE
    G2= 12VDC MAIN BATTERY
    G3= 12VDC ACCESSORIES POWER

    Correct Vehicle Voltage condition on these pins when ignition is OFF should be as follows:

    G1= 0VDC NEGATIVE
    G2= 12VDC MAIN BATTERY
    G3= 0VDC ACCESSORIES POWER
  • Unit Only Powers On When Reset Pin is Held

    The unit only comes on when the reset pin is pushed and held in and the optical light on the back comes on when the ignition is on.

    Solution:

    The accessories G3 is missing 12VDC. You have the following condition which needs to be fixed.

    Check for blown fuses, wire misalignment from factory pin to G plug or if the G3 accessories is only supplied by canbus (not all cases), the canbus could be faulty by not outputting 12VDC even if it has G2 main battery supply to it.

    G1= 0VDC NEGATIVE
    G2= 12VDC MAIN BATTERY
    G3= 0VDC ACCESSORIES POWER

    Below is an example of a symptom of 0VDC on G3 Accessories input.

  • Fuse Blowing When Unit Connected

    The fuse is blowing when the unit is connected, then the Negative G1 and Main Battery G3 are swapped.

    Solution:
    G1= 12VDC MAIN BATTERY
    G2= 0VDC NEGATIVE
    G3= 12VDC ACCESSORIES POWER
    Note: The unit has a 15A fuse, some vehicles accessories are lower than this and can blow. You may need to either upgrade the accessories fuse for your vehicle if it's safe to do so, or run a new accessories line.
  • Battery Drain Issue

    The unit turns off as expected by the battery is draining.

    Solution:

    Sometimes in some systems, the canbus (if installed) keeps something "awake" in the engine bay which drains the battery.

    The solution is to disconnect the G2 mains battery supply to the canbus, and power it by G3 accessories power only so that the canbus also turns off when the unit is off.

    Note: If the G3 only comes from the canbus only, then you will be required to run your own accessories power to the main power supply to the canbus instead.
    Important: Some installations with this issue have been identified as having the reverse camera incorrectly installed to the main battery G2 instead of the accessories. This will cause the camera to stay on all the time and drain the battery.
  • Screen Goes Black When Cranking Key

    When I crank the key from accessory to ignition, the HU screen goes black for 1-2 seconds and then comes back. Is this normal?

    Solution:

    This can happen if the vehicle is be cutting the accessories off when switching between accessories to ignition.

    You can go into general settings and increasing the "delay off" setting.

  • Unit Not Fast Booting

    My unit is not fast booting from start.

    Solution:

    Check in your Factory settings if you have Sleep Mode selected ON.

    In general settings, set sleep delay to 3 seconds.

  • USB Port Not Working

    My USB port / ports are not working.

    Solution:
    1. Check the connection for Port D (USB1) and E (USB2/3) and make sure they are pushed all the way in and seated correctly.
    2. Check the USB1 & USB2/3 cables for any damaged cables or loose wires.
    3. Check that the socket D and E pins are not bent or damaged.
    4. Measure the voltage on pins 1 to 3 on socket D and E for 5VDC. If you measure 5VDC then it is fine.
    5. If no voltage if found on these pins, try to perform a factory reset Settings - User - Backup/reset - 168 - clear all data.
    6. Unplug the canbus wait 5 minutes and plug it back in again.
    7. If still no success, send back for warranty repair.
    Note: If not using the supplied USB cables direct to the unit and instead using retention to the vehicles original USB port, this might not be compatible.
  • WiFi Not Staying On

    Whenever I start the unit the WIFI has to be turned on again. How do I keep the units WIFI on?

    Solution:

    Swipe the screen down to reveal the Wifi icon, Press and hold it to open the WIFI options and do the follow the below steps.

    1. Enter Wifi preferences.
    2. Turn on WIFI Automatically.
  • GPS Tracking Not Smooth/Accurate

    My GPS tracking of position is not as smooth/accurate as when I use Android Auto/Apple Carplay.

    Solution:

    You can check the GPS signal strength in the Settings --> Device --> GPS section.

  • Radio Automatically Mutes with Stop/Start

    I have an issue where I will be playing radio and it automatically mutes itself when the vehicle stop/start is triggered.

    Go to settings - Factory Settings - 168 - Start/Stop Enable.

  • Unit Cycles App Modes When Flicking Lights

    Solution:

Reverse Camera Issues

  • How to Connect LODARK Reverse Camera

    How do I connect the LODARK reverse camera?

    Solution:

    Make sure to not use crimp connectors, soldering is the best and most secure. There have been many reported issues with loosening or failed connections using these types.

    Please see for diagram and pinouts here.

  • OEM Reverse Camera Live View Not Working

    My OEM reverse camera works in reverse but not with live view.

    Solution:

    Live view is only supported by LODARK reverse camera.

  • LODARK Live View Working But Not in Reverse

    LODARK Live view is working but not when in reverse.

    Solution:

    You must check your connections, specifically the yellow reverse connection.

  • LODARK Reverse Camera Works in Reverse But Not Live View

    LODARK reverse camera works in reverse but not live view.

    Solution:

    You must check your connections, specifically the RED ACC connection.

  • LODARK Reverse Camera Black and White

    My LODARK reverse camera is black and white.

    Solution:

    Set the reverse camera is set to AHD in the factory settings (password 168).

  • Reverse Camera Not Working When Engine Started

    The reverse camera works when the engine is off. When I start my vehicle and put it in reverse, the camera does not work.

    Solution:

    This is caused by a voltage drop from the rear light, especially with long vehicles (Long cable run) and/or alot of work being done to it. Adding a relay for reverse light switching and taking power from the main battery or accessories power will resolve this issue.

  • Reverse Camera Static/Frozen Image

    The reverse camera is playing up, it displays static/frozen image on screen from time to time after shifting from reverse to forwarding. The static/frozen image will stay on the screen for 1 minute or more then back to normal.

    Solution:

    This is caused by lose connection on the Yellow RCA plug. Please check.

  • LODARK Reverse Camera Out of Focus/Blurry

    My LODARK reverse camera is showing out of focus/blury.

    Solution:

    This has been discovered as a damaged wiring harness during installation, especially at pinch points.

  • 360 Reverse Camera Delay

    LODARK 360 reverse camera has a delay before it shows up when I put it in reverse.

    Solution:

    REASON 1: You must have ALL 4 x 360 cameras installed. Not doing so will cause a delay on the rear 360 camera.

    REASON 2: You have a canbus and have not disconnected G5 (reverse detect) from it. This must be wired directly from the G plug to the Reverse Light power.

  • F Port Not Working with 360 System

    I have a CC3 360 or CC3 2K 360 and I don't want to use the 360 camera system. I want to install a camera using the F port but its not working.

    Solution:

    The F port is disabled in the 360 system. If you want to use the F port for a reverse camera when not installating the 360 camera system, you must install the non-360 firmware on your unit. Make sure to install the correct firmware 2K/Non-2K.

  • Mercedes Benz Vito 360 Camera Issue

    I have a Mercedes Benz Vito 3 W447 (2014-2020) and when I select the "trigger when turn" option OFF so that my CC3 360 does not trigger the side cameras when indicating, it still triggers.

    Solution:

    It was reported that updating the 4 x APKs resolved this issue.

Display & Audio Issues

  • OEM Amplifier Not Turning On

    I have OEM amplifier but its not turning on.

    Solution:

    If the supplied harness supports OEM amplifier, it should have a BLUE AMP wire. Connect this to either G3 Accessories, or to Blue AMP-C that is on the supplied CC3 AUX harness that has the sim card reader on it.

  • Screen Not Dimming with Head Lights

    My screen is not dimming and the side capacitive buttons are not illuminating when I turn the head lights on.

    Solution:

    Settings --> General --> Disable Backlight Control.

  • Screen Flickering Dark and Bright

    My screen is flickering dark and bright.

    Solution:

    If you have non-canbus safe LED that has replaced your glove box light and there is no glove box switch, change the LED back to the normal incandescent globe.

  • Screen Not Dimming to Settings

    My screen dim for head lights is in manual mode and no timer is active. My screen does not DIM to the settings I put when I turn my head lights on.

    Solution:

    If your instrument panel illumination also controls the dashboard lights illumination, then please check that your instrument panel illumination dial is turned up above its lowest level.

  • Setting Day/Night Screen Brightness

    How do I set my day time and night time screen brightness levels?

    Solution:

    Settings --> Device --> Display --> Brightness Level.

    My dynamic reverse guidelines are moving in opposite direction to my steering wheel.

    My OEM unit used to display my A/C, how can I do it?

  • Unit Not Sleeping

    My unit is not sleeping, but performing a full boot on start.

    Solution:

    Go to Settings --> Factory --> 168 --> if Sleep mode setting is off (to the left) toggle it on (to the right), If it is already ON (to the right) toggle it OFF and ON again.

  • Vehicle Brand Logo Not Showing

    This keeps popping up on my screen when changing launchers.

  • Change Time to 24hr Format

    How do I change my time settings to 24hr?

    Solution:

    Go to Settings --> System --> Date and Time --> Use 24-hour format.

  • GPS Stopped Working

    My GPS stopped working, how can I fix it?

    Solution:
    1. Check that the SMA connection at the rear of the head unit is not loose.
    2. Check for any cabling damage.
    3. Swipe the screen down and select "Reboot".
    4. If that does not work, perform a factory reset: Settings --> User --> Backup/Reset --> 168 --> Clear All Data.
    5. If each of those steps do not resolve the problem, replace the GPS dongle.
  • Microphone Sounds Quiet

    I sound very quiet with the internal mic, but also when I disable the internal mic and use the external microphone.

    Solution:

    A reported working solution is a factory reset: Settings --> User --> Backup/Reset --> 168 --> Clear All Data.

  • "str_release_space_fail" Prompt

    There is a "str_release_space_fail" prompt coming up on the bottom of the screen. What does this mean?

    Solution:

    This is for 360 systems. It means that the 360 recording video drive is full. You need to format it.

  • Unit Power Goes Out with Head Lights

    When ever I start my car the unit starts. When I turn on the head lights of my car the power to the head unit goes out. When I turn off the head light the power comes back to the head unit.

    Solution:

    We found that this issue was due to the connections not being connected properly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often is this troubleshooting guide updated?

This guide is continuously updated as we receive new information and identify additional issues. We recommend checking back periodically for the latest solutions.

What should I do if my issue isn't listed here?

If you can't find a solution for your specific issue, please contact our support team through the contact options below. Be sure to include as much detail as possible about your problem.

Are these solutions applicable to all LODARK models?

Most solutions apply to multiple models, but some may be specific to certain series or firmware versions. Always check your specific model and firmware version before applying any solutions.

Where can I find firmware updates for my unit?

Firmware updates are available in the Technology Portal section of our website. Make sure to download the correct firmware for your specific model and follow the installation instructions carefully.
