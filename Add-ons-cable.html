Application & Usage

Seamless Integration

Our interface plugs are designed for easy connection between your car's electrical system and aftermarket devices. They provide seamless integration for power cables and CAN bus systems, ensuring reliable performance and compatibility with your vehicle's electronics.

These plugs are essential for installing multimedia systems, navigation units, and other electronic accessories without compromising your car's original wiring.
